A Moment Under the Stars...

October 23, 2025 • Mood: Reflective

Last night felt unreal. I lay there, half-asleep, and the world loosened its edges. The ceiling became a sky and every small breath sounded like a distant drum. I wanted to hold the quiet, but the quiet pulled me into memory instead.

Sometimes the simplest sights make a flood — a streetlamp, a moth, a spilled cup of coffee. Tonight, it was a constellation I couldn't name. It felt like someone finally read the margins where I hide my deepest lines.
