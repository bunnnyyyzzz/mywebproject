My New Story

Last night felt surreal. The moonlight spilled across the room, and I could hear the soft whisper of the wind outside. Every moment seemed suspended in time, like the world itself had paused just for us. It was quiet, intimate, and filled with a strange kind of magic I had never felt before.

She came closer, and I could feel the warmth of her presence. I reached out my hand, expecting nothing, and she responded with a gentle smile that made my heart race. Every second that passed was electric, a moment I wanted to hold forever.
